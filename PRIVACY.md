# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

EmpowerHer ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Age, weight, height, and other health metrics you provide
- Medical history and symptoms you choose to share

### Usage Data
- Assessment results and predictions
- App usage patterns
- Device information

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide PCOS risk assessment services
- To generate personalized recommendations
- To improve our services and ML models
- To communicate with you about your account

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore with security rules
- We do not store raw PII in logs
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

## Data Retention

- Your assessment data is retained until you delete your account
- You can export your data at any time via the export endpoint
- You can delete your account and all associated data via the delete endpoint

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Export**: Download your data in JSON/CSV format
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Correction**: Update your profile information

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage
- No data is shared with third parties for marketing purposes

## Medical Disclaimer

**Important**: This application provides AI-based risk assessments for informational purposes only. It is not a medical diagnosis. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:
- Email: privacy@empowerher.app
